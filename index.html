Welcome to Quran Focus!

This app helps you focus on the Quran, one Ayah at a time.

Select a Surah and Ayah from the top bar to begin.

Listen and Reflect

Tap the screen once to play the audio recitation of the current Ayah.

Tap again to pause.

Navigate Ayahs

Swipe right to go to the next Ayah.

Swipe left to go to the previous Ayah.

Access Settings

Double-tap anywhere on the screen to quickly show or hide the top settings bar.

Control Your Experience

Use the top bar to change Surah/Ayah, view Ayah info (Juz, Page, Hizb), toggle fullscreen, or re-open this guide.
